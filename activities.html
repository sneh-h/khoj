Historical Tours

Mysore Palace, Karnataka

Explore the opulent Mysore Palace, an architectural marvel of the Wodeyar dynasty with stunning Indo-Saracenic architecture.

Qutub Minar, Delhi

Discover the Qutub Minar, the tallest brick minaret in the world, showcasing a significant example of Indo-Islamic architecture.

Hampi, Karnataka

Wander through the ruins of Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage site with ancient temples, palaces, and market streets.

Rajasthan Forts and Palaces

Experience the grandeur of Rajasthan's forts and palaces, including Amber Fort and Mehrangarh Fort, showcasing royal history.

Ajanta and Ellora Caves, Maharashtra

Explore the Ajanta and Ellora Caves, renowned for their ancient rock-cut temples and exquisite frescoes and sculptures.

Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Dive into the spiritual and historical significance of Varanasi, one of the world's oldest cities with temples and ghats.

Red Fort, Delhi

Visit the Red Fort, a massive fortification in Old Delhi, known for its red sandstone walls and historical significance.

Fatehpur Sikri, Uttar Pradesh

Explore Fatehpur Sikri, the former Mughal capital with well-preserved Mughal architecture and historical grandeur.

Elephanta Caves, Maharashtra

Discover the Elephanta Caves, famous for their Hindu and Buddhist rock-cut sculptures and ancient religious art.

Khajuraho Temples, Madhya Pradesh

Marvel at the Khajuraho Temples, known for their intricate erotic sculptures and architectural brilliance from the Chandela dynasty.
