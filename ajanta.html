Elephanta Caves

Explore the Ancient Elephanta Caves

Discover the Elephanta Caves, a network of sculpted caves located on Elephanta Island, east of the city of Mumbai. These rock-cut caves are renowned for their magnificent Hindu and Buddhist sculptures, which date back to the 5th to 8th centuries. Wander through the ancient rock temples and witness the intricate carvings that depict various deities and mythological themes.
