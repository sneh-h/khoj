Tours in Maharashtra

Elephanta Caves

Explore the Elephanta Caves, famous for their Hindu and Buddhist rock-cut sculptures and ancient religious art.

Ajanta and Ellora Caves

Discover the Ajanta and Ellora Caves, renowned for their ancient rock-cut temples and exquisite frescoes and sculptures.

Pune

Experience Pune's rich cultural heritage with attractions like Shaniwarwada, Aga Khan Palace, and the vibrant street food scene.

Mumbai

Explore Mumbai, the bustling city known for its landmarks like the Gateway of India, Marine Drive, and Bollywood.

Lonavala

Relax in Lonavala, a popular hill station with scenic views, waterfalls, and lush green landscapes.

Khandala

Discover Khandala, another charming hill station near Lonavala, famous for its serene environment and natural beauty.

Mahabaleshwar

Visit Mahabaleshwar, known for its breathtaking viewpoints, strawberry farms, and cool climate.

Alibaug

Enjoy a beach getaway in Alibaug, with its sandy shores, forts, and relaxing coastal ambiance.

Ratnagiri

Explore Ratnagiri, known for its scenic beaches, historical forts, and delectable seafood.

Panchgani

Relax in Panchgani, a serene hill station with picturesque landscapes and pleasant weather.
